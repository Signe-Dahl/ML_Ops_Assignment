In Aalborg, clouds prevail, 
Copenhagen's skies are grayest of all, 
Vodskov's mix is just right, 
making it the nicest place to be in tomorrow's light.
Copenhagen's chill is lost in clouds, 
Aalborg's breeze is crisp, but Vodskov's warmth is found.
Vodskov is the place to be, 
I morgen er Vodskov det bedste sted at være, 
der er en behagelig stemning.
