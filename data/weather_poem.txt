In Aalborg, clouds veil the sky, 
Copenhagen's grayness reigns high.
Vodskov's mix of sun and cloud, 
makes it the nicest, avowed.
Its balance of temperature and dew, 
makes it the place to be, anew.
Copenhagen's chill, Aalborg's cold, 
Vodskov's the best, to behold.

I Vodskov, sol og sky forener sig, 
det er den bedste by, at være i i morgen, 
med balance mellem temperatur og dug, 
Vodskov er det bedste valg, at nyde.
