English:
Clouds gather, temperatures sway, 
Aalborg's chill begins the day, 
Copenhagen's grey skies reign, 
Vodskov's balance makes it pleasant to remain.

Dansk:
Skyer samles, temperaturer svinger, 
Aalborgs kulde starter dagen, 
Københavns grå himmel hersker, 
Vodskovs balance gør det behageligt at blive.
