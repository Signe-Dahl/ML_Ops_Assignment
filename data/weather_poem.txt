English:
Copenhagen's clouds reign supreme, a grey domain,
Aalborg and Vodskov share a similar, chillier refrain.
Copenhagen's warmth beckons, a gentle, sunny breeze,
Head to Copenhagen, for a nicer tomorrow's ease.
