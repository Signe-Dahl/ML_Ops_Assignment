English:
Copenhagen's skies are veiled in grey,
Aalborg and Vodskov share a similar day,
But Copenhagen's warmth makes it the place to stay,
Tomorrow, its mild breeze will chase the blues away.

Dansk:
Københavns himmel er skjult i gråt,
Aalborg og Vodskov deler en lignende dag,
Men Københavns varme gør det til stedet at bo,
I morgen vil dens milde brise jage blues væk.
